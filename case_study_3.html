Alternative Dating

Identifying the gap: Looking at the record of Caravaggio's Bacchus on ArCo, we noticed that within the historicalInformation property, a date, now outdated, was given in relation to the creation of the painting.

Methodology: Searching within ArCo's ontology, we found the Alternative Dating property within the Context Description Ontology.

Results: As a result, linking the cultural asset to this property will make it easier to query the database for information.
