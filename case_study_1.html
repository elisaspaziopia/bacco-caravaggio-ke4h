Iconographic Description - Case Study 1

How we enriched the Knowledge Graph with Iconographic and Symbolic description.

Lack of a symbolic description

Identifying the gap: After examining the information sheet for Caravaggio's Bacchus on ArCo, we realized that although there is a description aimed at identifying the elements within the painting (by means of the dc:description and core:description properties), a symbolic interpretation is missing. This is an important aspect, often sought after when analyzing works of this kind.

Methodology: Unlike the properties mentioned above, we thought hasIconographicOrDecorativeApparatus property, being part of the Iconographic or Decorative Apparatus class (Denotative Description Ontology), could be the most suitable for the purpose of deepening the symbolic description of a cultural property. We wanted to understand the function of this property: taking a look at other resources that are linked to it is one of the best ways to choose wheter that property can be helpful to enrich a Knowledge Graph (in our case with the information extracted from LLMs). We decided to run the following query:

Query 1

View results in ArCo

Since it retrieves too many results, we decide to narrow down the research only to Historic or Artistic properties. We also add label to be able to see the name of the property.

Query 2

View results in ArCo

In this case, by taking a look at the results, it appears that this property is mostly linked to resources such as pieces of clothing, drawings and memorials. Since this property is widely used among resources that have the same classification as "Bacchus" by Caravaggio, we decide to ask LLMs (ChatGPT) to retrieve a triple out of this property, in order to enrich the Knowledge Graph (click on Prompting Techniques to see what LLMs told us about "Bacchus" by Caravaggio).

Triple✅

@prefix arco: https://w3id.org/arco/ontology/arco/ .
@prefix a-dd: https://w3id.org/arco/ontology/denotative-description/ .
@prefix rdfs: http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema# .
@prefix xsd: http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema# .
@prefix : https://example.org/resource/ .

https://dati.beniculturali.it/lodview-arco/resource/HistoricOrArtisticProperty/0900289176
    a arco:HistoricOrArtisticProperty ;
    a-dd:hasIconographicOrDecorativeApparatus :apparato0900289176 .

:apparato0900289176
    a a-dd:IconographicOrDecorativeApparatus ;
    rdfs:label "Apparato iconografico e decorativo 1 dell’opera 0900289176"@it ;
    a-dd:includesIconographicDescription :descrizione0900289176 .

:descrizione0900289176
    a a-dd:IconographicDescription ;
    rdfs:label "Descrizione iconografica"@it ;
    a-dd:hasInterpretationText """Il vino nella coppa rappresenta l'ebbrezza divina e terrena, l'alterazione dei sensi che avvicina al divino ma anche al peccato. Le guance arrossate e la presa malferma del calice, che genera increspature sulla superficie del vino, sembrano tradire un certo stato di ebbrezza. La frutta, pur simboleggiando abbondanza e fertilità, rivela imperfezioni significative: la mela è bacata, la mela cotogna è ammaccata, la pesca è mezza marcia, alludendo alla caducità della bellezza e del piacere terreno. La nudità parziale evoca sensualità carnale e disponibilità, mentre la corona di pampini richiama la tradizione dionisiaca dell'estasi mistica. La luce caravaggesca, che illumina drammaticamente la pelle del giovane contro lo sfondo scuro, crea un effetto di immediata presenza fisica, quasi tattile."""@it .

Validation of the query: After providing a set of instructions on how to generate the query—such as specifying the URI and the property we wanted to use—the LLM returned a result that was highly relevant and consistent with the ArCo ontology: the structure it used correctly follows ArCo’s modeling principles, for instance, the cultural property is first linked to an instance of a-dd:IconographicOrDecorativeApparatus, which then carries the descriptive content via core:hasDescription. This ensures semantic clarity and consistency within the ontology.

Result: Working hand in hand with artificial intelligence and providing it with more data related to existing resources on ArCo and the classes already present in its ontology makes it easier to achieve appropriate results. By adding this triple to ArCo, we achieved a more structured and semantically rich representation of the artwork’s iconographic and decorative apparatus. This enhancement improves data clarity, enables more precise queries, and facilitates interoperability within the ArCo knowledge graph. Furthermore, it allows for modular descriptions that can be easily extended or linked to other resources, supporting deeper analysis and richer interpretations.
