Lack of a symbolic description

Identifying the gap: Looking at the page of Caravaggio's Bacchus on ArCo, we realized that although there was a merely descriptive description of the elements within the painting (by means of the dc:description and core:description properties), there was a lack of symbolic and iconographic description, usually sought among the information regarding a work of art.

Methodology: Unlike the properties mentioned above, the hasIconographicDescription property, being part of the Iconographic or Decorative Apparatus Type class (Denotative Description Ontology), is the most suitable for the purpose of deepening the symbolic description of a cultural asset, consequently, also of the painting in question.

Results: As a result, not only is the file of the work more complete in terms of content, but also an enrichment of the possibilities of creating queries to query the database. It is suggested, however, for future developments, that the symbolic description be further developed and extended in such a way as to make it more structured.
